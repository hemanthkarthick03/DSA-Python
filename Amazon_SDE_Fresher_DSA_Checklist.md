## Study Strategy

### Week 1-2: Foundation
Focus on Easy problems from Arrays, Strings, and Linked Lists. Master basic operations and get comfortable with problem-solving patterns.

### Week 3-4: Core Data Structures
Dive into Trees, Stacks, Queues, and Hash Tables. Practice Medium difficulty problems.

### Week 5-6: Algorithms
Focus on Binary Search, Two Pointers, and basic Dynamic Programming problems.

### Week 7-8: Advanced Topics
Work on Graphs, advanced DP, and Heaps. Start tackling Hard problems.

### Week 9-10: Amazon Specific
Focus on problems marked with â­ (Amazon Favorites). Practice system design basics.

### Week 11-12: Mock Interviews
Practice with time constraints. Focus on explaining your approach clearly.

## Tips for Amazon Interviews

1. **Leadership Principles**: Be ready to discuss Amazon's leadership principles with examples
2. **Scalability**: Always think about how your solution scales
3. **Edge Cases**: Thoroughly test your solution with edge cases
4. **Communication**: Explain your thought process clearly
5. **Optimization**: Start with a working solution, then optimize
6. **Time Complexity**: Always analyze and state time/space complexity
